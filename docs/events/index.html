The Legend of DxCraft: Breath of the Conquest

We are happy to announce our 2nd official DxCraft Community tournament: The Legend of DxCraft: Breath of the Conquest! It will be a Conquest, 7vs7 tournament, featuring different gamemodes, such as Capture the Flag, King of the Hill or Team Deathmatch!



Information

  • The tournament format will be announced once we know the exact number of teams playing.

  • It will feature different Conquest gamemodes, all of them 7vs7.

  • We'll accept a maximum of 14 players and a minimum of 8 players per team. We will also be making wildcard teams, which are balanced teams made with players who complete a solo registration.

  • We will be using Discord for the tournament. Use this link to join, and once the registration closes we will be giving ranks to players and team leaders. You are required to use Discord for the tournament.

  • We are recruiting people for our staff team. If you want to be part of the team and help us out with the tournament, please fill the form below.

NOTE: The Staff on the event running department can't play the tournament.

Dates

  • Team registrations opens: Now!

  • Team registrations ends: November 6th, 23:59 CEST/17:59 EST.

  • Private servers release and wildcard registrations end: November 9th

  • Final format and bracket released: November 9th

  • Qualifiers and Group Stage: November 14th, 15th, 21st & 22nd.

  • Invitationals: November 28th & 29th.
