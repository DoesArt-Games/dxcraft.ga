The Legend of DxCraft: Breath of the Conquest

We are happy to announce our 2nd official DxCraft Community tournament: The Legend of DxCraft: Breath of the Conquest! It will be a Conquest, 7vs7 tournament, featuring different gamemodes, such as Capture the Flag, King of the Hill or Team Deathmatch!



Information

  • The tournament format will be announced once we know the exact number of teams playing.

  • It will feature different Conquest gamemodes, all of them 7vs7.

  • We'll accept a maximum of 14 players and a minimum of 8 players per team. We will also be making wildcard teams, which are balanced teams made with players who complete a solo registration.

  • We will be using Discord for the tournament. Use this link to join, and once the registration closes we will be giving ranks to players and team leaders. You are required to use Discord for the tournament.

  • We are recruiting people for our staff team. If you want to be part of the team and help us out with the tournament, please fill the form below.

NOTE: The Staff on the event running department can't play the tournament.

Dates

  • Team registrations opens: Now!

  • Team registrations ends: November 6th, 23:59 CEST/17:59 EST.

  • Private servers release and wildcard registrations end: November 9th

  • Final format and bracket released: November 9th

  • Qualifiers and Group Stage: November 14th, 15th, 21st & 22nd.

  • Invitationals: November 28th & 29th.

NOTE: Initially, the tournament will be played during 3 weekends. In case we believe 2 weekends fits better, we will push the start of the tournament to November 21st. Keep an eye on our #updates channel in Discord for more information.

Registrations

To register your team, go to our Events website and register your account by clicking on the bottom left icon. Aftrer that, click on the (+) icon at the left navbar. There you can:

  • Give your team a name & slug (short name for the URL)

  • Invite your teammates (you must have from 8 to 14 players on your team)

  • Disband your team in case you want to re-create it or no longer wish to participate in the TM.

NOTE: By creating your team you are automatically registered for the tournament.

Additionally, we will have Wildcard teams! A Wildcard team is formed with players that didn't find a team, but still want to compete in the tournament. By registering as a Wildcard player, you will be paired in a team with other Wildcard players, forming one or multiple teams that will be able to play the tournament. To inscribe yourself as a Wildcard player, please complete this form.

Maps, gamemodes & veto system

  • During October we will have a map testing period, in which you can submit your own Conquest maps and we will review them and add them to the considered map pool if we think they are great. Please, contact any Head of Events on Discord or tell us in if you wish to submit your map.

  • The final map pool will be based on the 5 Conquest gamemodes. Each gamemode could have two, one or zero maps. The following gamemodes are:

    • Team Deathmatch (TDM)
    • King of the Flag (KotF)
    • King of the Hill (KotH)
    • Capture the Flag (CTF)
    • 5-Capture Point (5CP)

  • Teams will be able to scrim those maps in the Scrims servers, and sumbit their feedback on Discord. Feedback is crucial if we want a solid map pool, so don't hesitate on telling us and/or the map maker! Our Gameplay team will be working really hard to end up with a solid, competitive map pool for this tournament.

  • The provisional map pool will be posted in #map-pool, and we will be posting periodic alerts on our Discord.

  • The final veto system will be released alongside the final format.

Tournament format

This tournament will be a 3 weeks tournament, running from the 14th of November until the 29th. We will post more information about the final format once the registrations close and we know the exact number of teams. However, we plan on using 2 weekends for the Qualifiers and the Group Stage, and 1 weekend for the Invitationals.

For the seeding process, the Event Planning team has been working really hard to finish the Inidividual Rating system. Each player received points based on their team's standing at the end of our last tournament. We've decided that those points will be part of the final seeding process for this tournament.

Keep an eye in the Events Discord for more information about the final seeding formula later this month, but it will inlcude different variables, such as Team Leader's seeding, Gameplay team's seeding and possibly ELO from our new Rankeds system, Bolt, given that it comes out of the Beta Season and gets an ELO reset before the tournament.

Servers and scrims

We currently have 8 scrims servers setup ready to scrim against other teams! To scrim a team:

  • Tag @Scrim Host in #scrim-host-tag in the events discord and one of our scrim hosts will open a server for your team to play in.

  • If the server is already opened, you can type /lfs [your-team] and it will broadcast a message to all scrims servers so other teams can see it. If you abuse this command its permission will be revoked and may end up in a punishment.

  • We will be having private servers for every team! Each team will receive a server to practice for the tournament, which can be started by any team member through the team's private discord channel. Only the team members will have permissions in-game. AFK players will be kicked after 10 minutes, and any server empty for more than 10 minutes will shut down automatically. Please note that the practice servers will have a player cap of 12 and is intended for strating, practicing defenses, cannons, etc.

  • Minecraft-Discord integration: In order to have access to their team's voice and text channels, players should link their Minecraft account to discord, using /discord link in-game.

Anticheat

As you probably know we are partnered with Badlion Client and their anticheat, BAC! The use of Badlion Client will me mandatory for all players. If you have any technical impediment and it's impossible for you to run BLC with BAC (i.e I use macOS and I can't use it) fill out this form and we will review your case. You will be required a screenshare prior to each day's tournament matches.

Prize pool, passes and server upgrades

  • The team leader of the winning team will receive a private server for the next tournament. They will have full permissions in-game.

  • A tournament winner rank both in-game and in the Discord server, with donor perks.

  • We will be selling Season Passes, Server upgrades and a special Tournament Supporter rank that will help us fund the prize pool, alongisde the brand new Overcast Cloak!

    • Server upgrade (5 USD): More slots and resources for a team's practice server of your choice.
    • Silver Pass (10 USD): Ability to spectate all tournament matches in-game until semifinals (not included).
    • Gold Pass (25 USD): Ability to spectate all tournament matches, including semifinals and Grand Final.
    • Tournament Supporter (35 USD): Gold Pass + Server upgrade + Special donator rank for OCC (2 months). Total value: 45 USD.
